Lolita Fashion

What is it?


Lolita fashion is an alternative street fashion that originated in Japan. The style is inspired by antique dolls, rococo and victorian fashion, and kawaii culture. While it's associated with the Harajuku neighborhood of Tokyo, it has spread worldwide, and you can find lolita communities in almost every country. While lolita fashion is mostly about the clothes, lolita fashion is commonly associated with ladylike activities, such as tea parties, museum trips, going to the ballet and opera, and picnics.


Why do you do it?


We dress up and have fun. Lolita fashion takes all your favorite things and throws it on a dress. The clothes bring joy to the wearer and to others. Lolita communities are also a great way to make friends and have people to join you on outings. While lolita is associated with ladylike activities, you can propose a meet up to do anything you want. With communities worldwide, it can help you find locals to consult or even hang out with on your vacation.
